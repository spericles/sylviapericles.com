Who am I?

Greetings!


I'm Sylvia and I recently obtained a Masters in Human-Computer Interaction from the State University of New York at Oswego. Before graduate school, I actually received my bachelors degree in Health Management.

My passion to help the people around me has cultivated my ability to focus on details, which is a great factor I have carried from my experience studying Health and now as a UX Designer.

Optimal user experience is an essential part of UX Design. I thinks that the ability to both optimize user experience while taking a minimalist approach can be a "game changer" in reaching a wide variety of audiences. I hope to be part of design teams that are enthusiastic about empowering people through better user experience.
