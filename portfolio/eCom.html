Hair Product E-commerce App

Visual Design  |  In progress



Overview

I am a hair enthusiast, and with natural hair it is difficult to find products that will work for your hair texture. After speaking to other natural hair enthusiast I realized that it would be great to find all the products that cater to our hair in one place. This application idea is to be presented to some programmer who will help me bring it to life. The goal of the application is to be able to gather all these products on Amazon and present it on the application. This will allowe users to purchase through the app using amazon credential.
Research to be conducted

Target User


Hair enthusiast, all genders.
